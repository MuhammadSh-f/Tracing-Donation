Who are we ?

We are nonprofit team
and work worldwide

Charities face a difficult funding environment, mainly because charities are not highly transparent. It has become difficult to know whether donations are reaching the right place, or taking another course, such as financing suspicious campaigns, terrorist projects, etc. As a result, donors lose faith in these organizations, leading to a halt to interactions with charitable organizations. It makes it more difficult for charities to raise money at a great cost. Our project idea solves this problem using Blockchain, a decentralized database that provides security, transparency, and lower funding cost by eliminating the presence of third parties between donors and charities. All donations can be tracked in Blockchain, allowing donors to know where and how their money is being used. All donations are made using smart contracts, which allow donors to know exactly when and how their donations will be received. Blockchain technology saves time, reduces the cost of donation, and reduces the risk of reaching suspicious campaign donations or terrorist projects. Through this solution, a safe and easy donation environment is provided to increase the demand for donations and participate in charity.
